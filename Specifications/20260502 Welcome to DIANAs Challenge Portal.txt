Welcome to DIANA’s Challenge Portal!
We are currently inviting companies from across the Alliance to submit proposals to our Decision Superiority for NATO Warfighters Challenge through this portal. This challenge has been formed to address urgent priorities of NATO and Allied nations.

Successful applicants will become DIANA innovators and will receive contractual funding to enable demonstration of their solutions to NATO and its Allies.

To submit a proposal, you must create a new user account and company profile. Please note, you must register your user account in an authenticator application using the QR code provided. Please refer to the FAQs for more information.

If you have an inquiry, please review the FAQs. If your inquiry is not addressed by the FAQs, please raise a support ticket at the bottom of the FAQs page.

Please note that DIANA will also continiue to run its flagship annual Challenge Call in June 2026, consisting of a series of challenges, allowing selected innovators to undertake DIANA's Accelerator Programme.
